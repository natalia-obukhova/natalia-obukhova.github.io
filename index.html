Natasha Obukhova

HCI researcher in supporting grief in unconventional ways

Research

My research focuses on the design of technology to support grief in unconventional ways. I am interested in how technology can be used to support the grieving process, and how it can be used to help people cope with loss. I am particularly interested in how games can be used to support bereaved people and how implications from game design can be utilized in designing conventional grief support apps.
